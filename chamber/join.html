Membership Options

Non-Profit Organization

We provide a special offer for any non-profit organization. This option includes all of the benefits of a silver membership but at no fee.

Bronze Membership

The bronze membership offers you the basics. This also includes a 10% discount at all particpating businesses in the area as well as an invitation to attend 10 of our training conferences per year. This costs $500/year.

Silver Membership

The silver Membership offers the basics and a little extra. This includes a 20% discount at all particpating businesses in the area, invitations to attend 10 of our training conferences per year, and advertising for your business including on our website and monthly newsletter. This costs $800/year.

Gold Membership

Gold membership you will recieve all basic benefits but you will also be given VIP status. This offers a 25% discount at all particpating businesses in the area, invitations to attend all training conferences throughout the year, prefered VIP seating and lounge at conferences, exclusive invite to VIP club dinner, and spotlight advertising for your business on our website homepage and monthly newsletter. This costs $1500/year.
